Mobile Web Developer contractor is needed to provide support to our wireless telecommunications client in Walnut Creek, CA.

Requirements:

  • Front End & Mobile App Developer responsible for front-end web development and application mobilization to iOS and Andriod

Qualifications:

  • Must have 3+ years of development experience
  • Must have experience with Java, REST, JSON, HTML5, CSS, Javascript, Ajax, Mobile Toolkits
  • Solid working knowledge of Android SDK using Android APIs with JSON/REST & Hibernate
  • Solid working knowledge of iOS, XCode & DB conne ctivity through XCode
  • Must have experience in mobile application framework
  • Must have understanding of MVC architecture model

Additional Information:

We are unable to sponsor H1B Visa’s and/or subcontract to Third Party Vendors. ONLY W2 contractors should apply.

OUTSOURCE provides great benefits to our contractors: Access to Pre-tax Benefits, i.e. Healthcare, Commuter benefits, Flexible Spending Account (FSA), 401(k) Savings Plan, and Direct Deposit.
