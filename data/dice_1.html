Seeking a CNC Service Technician/Engineer with 3-5 years of experience in mechanical and electrical maintenance and repair. The ideal candidate will have a machine tools background primarily working with Japanese CNC machines.



We are a leading technology provider of advanced machining solutions to North American metal-cutting manufacturers and their affiliates. We are committed to the survival and growth of the American manufacturing industry. Our priority is to introduce technologies that strengthen our customers' ability to compete in the markets they serve.

What's In It for You

- Competitive salary ($35,000 - $70,000)

- Car allowance and leased vehicles.

- Benefits include Life Insurance, Medical insurance and 401k.

What You Will Be Doing

Service calls that will require repair, maintenance and customer support of CNC mill and lathe machines. Replacement of spindles, ball screws, bearing, linear guideways, hydraulics, pneumatics and alignments. Diagnosing alarms, troubleshooting ladder or other logic, backup or loading of software, control networking to computer, replacing motors, drivers, encoders, scales, solenoid vales, relay fuses and pumps. Interacting with our operations and service departments on your customer visits. Providing routine service and reports to management.

What You Need for this Position

1) Prior experience in mechanical/electrical maintenance on CNC machine tools background, primarily in Japanese product lines.

2) Knowledge and troubleshooting of CNC controls including: Siemens, Heidenhain, Fanuc or Mitsubishi.

3) Ability to work without supervision and provides routine service reports to management. Must have excellent customer service and communication skills.

4) Has worked with manufacturers, suppliers, subcontractors and multiple customers.

5) Has a strong technical aptitude, analytical and technology based skills with Mechanical Engineering and/or Electrical Engineering background is a plus as is understanding of Customer Support.

6) Valid driver's license is required (this is a field position and you will be traveling to clients in the area).So, if you are a CNC Service Technician/Engineer with strong knowledge of setup, repair and maintenance please apply today!

Applicants must be authorized to work in the U.S.
