Position Description

IT Specialist - Secret Clearance Job





Secret Cleared IT Specialist needed for a contract opportunity with Yoh's client located in Yuma, Arizona.


The Big Picture - Top Skills You Should Possess:


    - A+, Security + or Network + Certification



What You'll Be Doing:


    - Under broad direction, oversee and perform advanced systems installation, operation, integration, and troubleshooting in order to maintain optimum information technology system operations.
    - Design and oversee upgrades and new installs of information technology systems and services ensuring coordination with Cyber Security Specialist.
    - Perform Tier III upgrades of networking systems, information technology systems, and storage networking.
    - Perform Tier III troubleshooting, optimization, and performance management of Internet Protocol, Domain Name System (DNS), Dynamic Host Configuration Protocol (DHCP), and routing protocols.
    - Integrate enterprise, regional, and local IT systems ensuring current network operations are sustained or oversee their recovery.



What You Need to Bring to the Table:


    - Knowledge of Internet Protocol version 6 (IPv6), Enhanced Interior Gateway Routing Protocol (EIGRP), Border Gateway Protocol (BGP), Virtual Local Area Network (VLAN), Virtual Private Network (VPN), and Network Address Translation (NAT).
    - Ten (10+) years of experience required.
    - Possess a Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) or Cisco Certified Design Professional (CCDP), Microsoft Technology Associate (MTA) or a Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (MCTS) certification.
    - High school Diploma or General Equivalency Diploma (GED).
    - 8570 Certification: IAT 2: GSEC, Security+, or SSCP.
    - ES Certs: A+, MTA or MCTS.
